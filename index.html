About the Blog

A Love Letter to
Nigerian Weddings

Aso Ebi was born from a deep love of Igbo wedding culture — the vibrant colours, the sacred rituals, the music that makes your whole body move, and the joy of a community coming together to celebrate love.

This blog documents and celebrates the beauty of Nigerian weddings — from Igba Nkwu wine-carrying ceremonies and Iku Aka bride price traditions, to Aso Ebi fashion, bridal beauty, and everything in between.
