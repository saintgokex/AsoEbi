About the Blog

A Love Letter to
Nigerian Weddings

Aso Ebi was born from a deep love of Igbo wedding culture — the vibrant george fabrics, the sacred rituals of the Igba Nkwu, the highlife music that fills the compound, and the profound joy of a whole community coming together to witness love.

This blog is dedicated to documenting, celebrating, and sharing the beauty of Nigerian weddings — from Igbo wine-carrying ceremonies and bride price traditions, to Aso Ebi fashion, bridal beauty, and food that feeds a village.
